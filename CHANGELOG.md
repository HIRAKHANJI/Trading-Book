# Changelog

All notable changes to The Trading Playbook are documented here.

## [4.0.0] -- 2026-03-31

### Added
- Expanded to **42 chapters** (11 new chapters added)
- **Interactive quiz system** embedded throughout chapters with progress tracking and localStorage persistence
- **In-app trading ledger** with performance stats (win rate, average R:R, P&L, streaks) and CSV export
- Social discovery workflow chapter
- Iman's categorical trading method chapter
- Prop firm guide and evaluation chapter
- On-chain analysis chapter
- Order book and tape reading chapter
- Funding rates chapter
- Tax and record-keeping chapter
- Automation basics chapter

### Changed
- PWA update detection via `postMessage` -- users are notified when new content is available
- All external links made clickable and functional
- Curriculum restructured as crypto-first with forex as a secondary track

## [3.0.0] -- 2026-03-30

### Added
- Expanded to 31 chapters with comprehensive 12-week curriculum structure
- Wyckoff method chapter (accumulation/distribution schematics, composite man, springs and upthrusts)
- SMC/ICT concepts chapter (order blocks, fair value gaps, Judas Swing, liquidity sweeps)
- Macro and fundamental analysis chapter (FOMC, CPI, NFP, correlation to crypto)
- Obsidian trading ledger integration guide with Dataview queries
- Go/No-Go live trading evaluation checklist

### Changed
- Reorganized all content into Foundation, Phase 1-3, and Reference sections
- Platform setup expanded to include SMRT Algo and Naga Demo

## [2.0.0] -- 2026-03

### Added
- Chart patterns chapter (triangles, flags, head and shoulders, double tops/bottoms)
- Fibonacci retracements and extensions chapter
- Volume profile and market profile chapter
- Session analysis chapter (London, New York, Tokyo, overlap windows)
- Backtesting methodology chapter with structured workflow
- Multiple timeframe analysis chapter

### Changed
- Expanded candlestick content with additional patterns
- Improved support/resistance chapter with dynamic S/R concepts

## [1.0.0] -- 2026-03
